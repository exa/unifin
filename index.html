How it works
The Viability Assurance Currency is calculated on the basis of the four factors that underpin the human existence, and the methodology is open to the addition of new factors of influence.

INDEX Water Resource
INDEX Land Fertility
INDEX Forest Land

Viability INDEX
The Viability INDEX is calculated on the basis of weighted average values of influence factors. It is immune to market influences. Used as a Single Value Equivalent.
VAC
The Viability Assurance Currency the world's first government-backed crypto currency. VAC is not just a mean of payment, but a VACcine against the chaos of the global economy.
